### implementing a queue mechanism to buffer the requests  to overloaded kafka queue
 
Approach to implementing a queue mechanism for buffering requests to an overloaded Kafka queue

Let's begin the interview:

Hello! I understand you're working on implementing a queue mechanism to buffer requests to an overloaded Kafka queue. This is an interesting challenge that many organizations face. Could you walk me through your approach and why you believe it's necessary?

I'd like to understand:

- What specific symptoms led you to determine the Kafka queue is overloaded
- The architecture you're considering for your buffering solution
- How you plan to handle backpressure and rate limiting
- Your strategy for monitoring and managing this additional buffering layer
