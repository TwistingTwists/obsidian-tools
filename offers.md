To create an offer here are three things to think about. 

1. target audience 
2. Where they are at in their current journey 
3. where do they want  to be 

---
Use this framework to create offers for specific target audience. Narrow down target audience as much as possible. 



**Targeted Offers for Specific Audiences**
 
**1. New Parents**

* **Target Audience:** First-time mothers aged 25-35
* **Current Journey:** Overwhelmed and exhausted from caring for a newborn
* **Desired Outcome:** Reduced stress and improved sleep
* **Offer:** "New Parent Survival Kit" - a package of baby care products, self-care essentials, and a sleep consulting session.

**2. Fitness Beginners**

* **Target Audience:** Busy professionals aged 25-45 who want to lose weight and get fit
* **Current Journey:** Frustrated by lack of progress and uncertain about how to start a workout routine
* **Desired Outcome:** Motivation and a structured fitness plan
* **Offer:** "Get Fit in 30 Days" - a 30-day online fitness program with personalized coaching and support.

**3. Small Business Owners**

* **Target Audience:** Solo entrepreneurs aged 30-50 who struggle with marketing and customer engagement
* **Current Journey:** Feeling overwhelmed by the lack of marketing expertise and struggling to attract customers
* **Desired Outcome:** Increased website traffic and more consistent leads
* **Offer:** "Marketing Masterclass" - a 6-week online course teaching marketing fundamentals, including social media, SEO, and email marketing.

**4. Students**

* **Target Audience:** College students aged 18-22 who need help managing stress and improving grades
* **Current Journey:** Feeling overwhelmed by academic demands and struggling to keep up with coursework
* **Desired Outcome:** Improved time management and study skills
* **Offer:** "Study Hacks and Productivity Tools" - a one-on-one coaching session to help students optimize their study routine and achieve better grades.

**5. Home Sellers**

* **Target Audience:** Homeowners aged 35-55 considering selling their property
* **Current Journey:** Uncertain about the selling process and concerned about getting a good price
* **Desired Outcome:** A smooth and successful sale with minimal stress
