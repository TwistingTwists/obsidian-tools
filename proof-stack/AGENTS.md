## Naming Pattern

```
YYYY-MM-DD-###-document-title.md
```

- **YYYY-MM-DD** = Date created
- **###** = Sequential number (001, 002, 003, etc.)
- **document-title** = Kebab-case

**Example:** `2026-01-27-005-decision-maker-materials.md`

---

## Numbering Scheme

- **001-004** = Foundation (What/Design/How-to/Plan)
- **005-010** = Decision-maker & organization
- **011-020** = Blog posts, case studies, videos
- **021-030** = Quarterly reports
- **031+** = Future additions
