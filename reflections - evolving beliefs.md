## Table 2: Structured Exploration – Deep Reflective Questions

|Reflection Step|Reflective Question|My Reflection|
|---|---|---|
|**Clarifying My Belief**|What exactly do I believe about this?||
||Why does this belief matter to me?||
||What does holding this belief give me (comfort, identity, protection)?||
|**Origin & Influences**|When and how did I first adopt this belief?||
||Who or what influenced me to think this way?||
||Which experiences reinforced or challenged this belief over time?||
|**Evidence & Truth Testing**|What facts or evidence do I have to support this belief?||
||What evidence or experiences exist against this belief?||
||Have I intentionally sought evidence that contradicts this belief? Why or why not?||
|**Alternative Perspectives**|How would someone who completely disagrees see this belief?||
||If I remove my personal bias or emotions, how might I see it differently?||
||What perspective have I avoided considering, and why?||
|**Implications & Consequences**|How has this belief shaped my behaviors, decisions, relationships, and life trajectory?||
||If I continue holding this belief, where might it lead me?||
||What might I lose or gain if I abandon or change this belief?||
|**Behavioral Patterns (Distant)**|Can I recall past incidents (years ago) driven by this belief? What happened?||
||How did that past behavior shape my beliefs or identity today?||
|**Behavioral Patterns (Recent)**|What recent actions (days/weeks) reflect this belief?||
||Was there inconsistency in my behavior compared to my stated belief? Why?||
|**Deeper Truths & Vulnerabilities**|What fear, hope, or vulnerability lies beneath this belief?||
||Is there a deeper truth or emotion I’ve been avoiding through this belief?||
||What might I have to face if this belief proves false?||
|**Belief Adjustment & Integration**|How could I reframe this belief in a healthier or more balanced way?||
||What actionable experiment can I run to test this reframed belief?||
||How might this adjusted belief impact my behavior or decisions moving forward?||
