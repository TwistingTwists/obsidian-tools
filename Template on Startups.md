### a. SWOT Analysis

For each idea, evaluate:

- **Strengths:**
    - What are the core advantages of the idea?
- **Weaknesses:**
    - What limitations or challenges exist?
- **Opportunities:**
    - What market trends or gaps can the idea exploit?
- **Threats:**
    - What external factors or competitors might hinder success?

### b. Value Proposition Canvas

- **Customer Segments & Jobs-to-be-Done:**
    - Identify which idea best aligns with a clear customer need.
- **Pain Relievers & Gain Creators:**
    - Determine which solution effectively alleviates customer pain and delivers clear benefits.

### c. Market Opportunity Matrix

- **Market Size & Growth:**
    - Estimate the market size for each idea and the potential growth.
- **Competitive Landscape:**
    - Evaluate the number of competitors and the intensity of competition.
- **Revenue Potential:**
    - Project the revenue potential based on market research and initial tests.
